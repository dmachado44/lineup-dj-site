Built by a coach and
product designer

Hi, I'm Dan Machado, the creator of Lineup DJ.

I've been designing digital products and apps for over 12 years, helping teams build software used by millions of people. But Lineup DJ started from something much closer to home — coaching my son's baseball team.

After three seasons of coaching youth baseball, starting back in tee-ball, I saw how much kids love the little moments that make the game feel special. Walkout songs and player introductions can turn a normal at-bat into something the kids remember all season.

The Idea

There were already some great apps that helped teams run walkout music during games, but as a parent and volunteer coach I often found myself wishing the experience was a little easier and more intuitive for families managing a team.

As a product designer, I naturally started noticing opportunities to improve the user experience.

So I decided to build something that was designed specifically for parents and youth coaches.

Why Lineup DJ Exists

Lineup DJ was created to make it easy for parents and coaches to create big-league moments for kids.

With Lineup DJ you can:

  • Add walkout songs for every player
  • Create announcer-style player introductions
  • Share your team with parents so everyone can set up their own player
  • Run your lineup seamlessly during the game

All from one simple app designed specifically for youth baseball and softball teams.

The goal is simple: help kids feel like big leaguers for a moment.

Built for Youth Sports

Lineup DJ is used by parents and coaches across youth sports including Little League, travel baseball, coach pitch, tee-ball, and youth softball.

If you've ever watched a kid light up hearing their name announced before stepping up to the plate, you know exactly why this app exists.

Independent Builder

Lineup DJ is an independent project built and maintained by one person.

I'm constantly improving the app based on feedback from parents and coaches using it during real games. If you have ideas, suggestions, or feedback, I'd love to hear from you.

Let's make game day more fun

At the end of the day, youth sports are about creating memories.

If Lineup DJ can help make even a few of those moments more special for kids and their families, then it's doing its job.

Lineup DJ

Lineup DJ makes it easy to add walkout songs and player introductions to youth baseball and softball games. Coaches and parents can quickly set up walkup music for every player and run their lineup effortlessly on game day.
