Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an Apple Music subscription in order for Lineup DJ to work?

Yes, Lineup DJ requires an active Apple Music subscription to search for and play walkout songs. The AI voice introductions work independently of the music subscription.

What sports is Lineup DJ best for?

Lineup DJ is great for any sport where player introductions and walkout songs enhance the game-day atmosphere, such as baseball, softball, basketball, hockey, and more!

How does the AI voice work?

Lineup DJ uses advanced text-to-speech technology to create natural-sounding voice introductions for each player based on the names you enter in the lineup.
