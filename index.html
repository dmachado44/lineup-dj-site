Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 7-day free trial work?
When you sign up for Lineup DJ Pro, you get full access to every feature for 7 days — completely free. No credit card is required to start. If you decide not to continue, simply cancel before the trial ends and you won't be charged anything.
Do players need an Apple Music subscription?
The person running Game Day mode needs an active Apple Music subscription on their device — that's typically the parent or coach running the app during the game. Individual players don't need their own subscriptions.
Can I share my lineup with another coach or parent?
Yes! With Lineup DJ Pro, you can generate a shareable link for your lineup. Anyone with the link can import it directly into the app on their device. Links expire after a set time for security.
How do the AI announcer intros work?
When you generate an intro for a player, our AI voice engine creates a professional stadium-announcer style recording using that player's name and details. It sounds like the big leagues — and it only takes a few seconds to generate.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Absolutely. You can cancel at any time from your account settings. You'll continue to have access to Pro features until the end of your current billing period. No questions asked.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards — Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover — processed securely through Stripe. All payments are handled through Apple's in-app purchase system on iOS.
Does it work for both baseball and softball?
Yes! Lineup DJ works for any youth sport where players walk up one at a time — baseball, softball, tee-ball, you name it. The walkout experience is the same regardless of sport.
What happens if I'm at a field with no cell service?
We know fields don't always have great connectivity. AI intros are cached on your device after generation, so they'll play offline. Songs require an Apple Music connection to stream, so we recommend setting up before arriving at the field on Wi-Fi when possible.
