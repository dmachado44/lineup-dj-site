Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an Apple Music subscription for Lineup DJ to work?

Yes. Lineup DJ uses Apple Music to search for and play walkout songs. An active Apple Music subscription, including the free trial, is required to add and play songs during games.

Do all parents need to pay for Lineup DJ?

No. Only one person on the team needs a paid Lineup DJ subscription to run Gameday mode during games. Parents can join the team and set up their player's walkout song and introduction for free.

Can parents add their player's walkout song themselves?

Yes. Coaches can share their team with parents using a simple invite code. Each parent can then add their player's walkout song and introduction so the lineup stays updated automatically.

What sports is Lineup DJ best for?

Lineup DJ is designed primarily for youth baseball and softball teams, including Little League, travel ball, coach pitch, and tee-ball. Any sport that uses player introductions or walkout music can also use Lineup DJ.

How do walkout songs work in youth baseball?

Walkout songs are short clips of music that play when a player walks up to bat. With Lineup DJ, coaches can assign a walkout song to each player and trigger the music instantly during the game.

Can I choose the exact part of the walkout song that plays?

Yes. Lineup DJ lets you trim the exact moment of a song so players hear their favorite part when they walk up to the plate.

Does Lineup DJ include player introductions?

Yes. Lineup DJ includes announcer-style player introductions that play before the walkout song, making every at-bat feel like a big-league moment for youth players.

Can Lineup DJ run the full batting lineup during a game?

Yes. Coaches can run the entire lineup from the app, trigger player introductions, and play walkout music for each batter throughout the game.

How do I move a player to the bench?
  • Go to your Team
  • Find the player in your lineup
  • Swipe left on the player’s row
  • Tap “Bench” to move them to the bench

To bring them back:

  • Find the player on the bench
  • Swipe left on their row
  • Tap “Active” to move them back into the lineup
Why isn’t my player’s song playing?

Lineup DJ requires an active Apple Music subscription to play songs.

  • Make sure you’re logged into Apple Music on your device
  • Confirm you have an active subscription or free trial
  • Try playing the song directly in Apple Music to verify it works
  • If needed, go to Settings in Lineup DJ and tap “Restore Purchases”
